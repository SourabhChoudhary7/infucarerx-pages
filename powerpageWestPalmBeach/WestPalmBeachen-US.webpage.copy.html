WELCOME

Dear Customer:

Thank you for allowing InfuCare Rx the opportunity to serve you. Please read through the materials provided in your Patient Admission Packet, in order to help you become more familiar with our services and the therapy your doctor has ordered for you.

The InfuCare Rx Patient Admission Packet contains information on how to contact us, how we protect your privacy and disclose healthcare information, emergency preparedness and safety tips, medication instructions specific to your treatment, and other important information that will allow us to properly care for you. Additionally, we have provided you with instructions on completing and returning the Patient Admission Agreement, Assignment of Benefits, Privacy Notice, and Patient Satisfaction Survey enclosed in your folder. Please call our pharmacy at (877) 469-6053 if there is anything you do not understand in this packet.

InfuCare Rx's dedicated professionals are committed to ensuring that the services you receive are provided in a safe and effective manner. As our valued customer, your safety, well-being and satisfaction is of the utmost importance to us.

Our healthcare team is comprised of clinicians and healthcare professionals. Prior to admission to our services, you will receive a phone call from our office. We will review information with you regarding our Patient Management Program, perform an on- boarding assessment, explain your plan of treatment, and discuss ways that you can participate in your care plan. Your progress will be monitored throughout your treatment, while providing on-going communication to you, your doctor, insurance company and support team.

Patient Management Program:

InfuCare Rx's Patient Management Program is designed to help you gain access to services to improve your health and quality of life. Our dedicated team of pharmacists, nurses and other trained professionals create a collaborative approach to care to ensure your therapy is provided in a safe and effective manner. This is achieved through communication, education and close clinical monitoring throughout your therapy resulting in high-quality, cost-effective outcomes.

You will be automatically enrolled in our Patient Management Program upon admission to service. We ask that you or a caregiver actively participate in our program which will help to ensure you receive the full benefits of our services.

Components of our program include, but are not limited to the following:

  • Access to individualized or patient specific services
  • Information on community resources and support
  • Effective communication with you, your caregiver, prescriber, and other members of your care team regarding your treatment plan and progress throughout your therapy
  • Education and clinical monitoring related to your condition, medications, adherence, side effects, food and drug interactions, safe disposal of medications, and other safety precautions

Please contact our pharmacy department if you choose not to participate in our program or have additional questions regarding your care (877) 469-6053.

Patient Financial Responsibility:

InfuCare Rx will verify your insurance coverage for the services we are requested to provide, prior to performing any service. Your out-of-pocket costs, copays, deductibles and your financial responsibility, including if InfuCare Rx is out-of-network with your insurance company, will be communicated to you verbally and in writing. This information will be explained to you prior to your treatment and provided to you in writing in the Assignment of Benefits (AOB) document provided in your admission folder.

Pharmacy Process and Communication:

Product Selection: If your product is not available from InfuCare Rx, we can attempt to order product from a wholesaler. If we cannot obtain it or you are unable to wait for the wholesaler's delivery, we will call other pharmacies in the area on your behalf.

Refilling Prescriptions: If certain products are limited by your insurance companies benefit design, we can offer you a cash price and/or help you explore assistance programs.

Placing an OrderInfuCare Rx pharmacy staff will call you prior to your next refill and will perform an assessment of your needs within an adequate amount of time before your next refill so that you do not run out of medication or supplies. If you wish to place an order ahead of this, you can call our pharmacy Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM at (877) 202-6017.

Accessing Medications in Emergency, Disaster or Delay: InfuCare Rx has a plan in place so that we are prepared to care for you in the event of a natural disaster or emergency. Emergency preparedness tips have been provided to you in your admission packet.

Order Status: If for some reason we cannot provide a healthcare service for you, your prescriber will be notified within 5 days or sooner. You can also call our pharmacy during normal business hours of operations to inquire about when your order will be delivered. Currently, there is no method to check the status of your order on our website. If there are any delays in processing your order or delivery, we will notify you by phone.

Substitution of Medications: Our pharmacy may consult with your prescriber to recommend a different medication/therapy in the event of an insurance formulary issue, prohibitive cost, or therapeutic need (based on lab results or clinical response). "Law permits pharmacists to substitute a less expensive generically equivalent drug for a brand name drug unless you or your physician direct otherwise."

Transfer of Prescriptions: If you decide to use another pharmacy, InfuCare Rx will contact the new pharmacy and verbally transfer the prescription to the pharmacy of your choice, along with any pertinent information such as insurance and demographic information.

Flying with Medication: If you will be traveling by airplane with your medications, you may need to contact the Transportation Security Administration (TSA) before your travel date to request assistance. In most cases your request should be made 72 hours in advance of your departure. For additional information you may call TSA Cares hotline at 855-787-2227, visit www.tsa.gov, or call our pharmacy.
